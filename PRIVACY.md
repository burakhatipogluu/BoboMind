# BoboMind Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 26, 2026

## Overview

BoboMind is a clipboard history manager for macOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data BoboMind accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Collection

**BoboMind does NOT collect, transmit, or share any data.** Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — BoboMind contains no analytics frameworks, telemetry, or usage tracking of any kind.
- **No network requests** — BoboMind does not connect to the internet. All functionality is entirely offline.
- **No third-party SDKs** — BoboMind includes no third-party code that could collect data.

## Data Storage

- **Clipboard history** is stored locally on your Mac using SwiftData (Core Data).
- **All data remains on your device** and is never transmitted anywhere.
- Data is stored within the app's sandboxed container.

## Clipboard Access

BoboMind monitors the system clipboard to save your copy history. This is the core functionality of the app. The clipboard data is:

- Stored only on your local device
- Never sent to any server or third party
- Protected by macOS App Sandbox

## Password Manager Protection

BoboMind automatically excludes copies from known password managers (1Password, Bitwarden, LastPass, Dashlane, KeePassXC, Enpass, NordPass, Apple Passwords) to protect sensitive credentials. You can also manually exclude any app in Settings.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your clipboard history at any time:

- **Clear All (Keep Pinned)** — removes all clips except pinned ones
- **Clear Everything** — removes all clips including pinned ones
- **Uninstalling** the app removes all stored data

## Children's Privacy

BoboMind does not collect any data from anyone, including children.
